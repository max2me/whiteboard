WHITEBOARD

This is an experiment to bring the best parts of whiteboarding to computers that do not have pen or touch input.

BASICS

  • Draw with left mouse button
  • Erase with right mouse button
  • Double click and start typing for text
  • Ctrl+Shift+Click to create polyline
  • Ctrl+Drag to resize
  • Shift+Drag to move
  • Alt+Drag to clone
  • DEL to delete

You can only modify most recent object that you've drawn by either resizing/moving/cloning or specifying its shape.
